Tomato Egg Dish

  • 5 eggs
  • 3 tomatoes
  • water, ketchup, salt, and sugar to taste (go light on the sugar then add as you go), and cornstarch slurry
    1. Combine eggs and scramble to your likeness then take out eggs
    2. Add neutral cooking oil then add in tomatoes and stir fry for 30sec - 1minute
    3. Add some water until it becomes a light soup consistency and add in cornstarch slurry
    4. Add some ketchup and eggs, mix, then add sugar and salt to taste
